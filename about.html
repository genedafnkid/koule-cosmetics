changing the ideal beauty standards

Welcome to Koule Cosmetics, a small business based in the Philippines. Our mission is to change the ideal beauty standards, and we do this by offering high-quality and sustainable cosmetic products that cater to everyone. We believe that beauty should be inclusive and accessible to everyone, and our products reflect this philosophy. From our natural and eco-friendly Koule Brow Soap to our innovative Powder Up Tints, we aim to help people feel confident and beautiful in their own skin. We are committed to sustainability, cruelty-free and vegan products, and supporting local businesses. Thank you for choosing Koule Cosmetics and being a part of our journey towards changing the beauty industry for the better.

MISsion

Our mission at Koule Cosmetics is to empower people to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin. We strive to change the ideal beauty standards by offering high-quality and sustainable cosmetic products that are inclusive and accessible to everyone. We are committed to using natural and eco-friendly ingredients, supporting local businesses, and providing cruelty-free and vegan options. Our goal is to help people embrace their unique beauty and express themselves through our innovative products.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading sustainable cosmetic brand in the Philippines, known for our commitment to changing the beauty industry for the better. We envision a world where beauty is not defined by society's standards but is celebrated in all its forms. We aspire to create a positive impact on the environment by using natural and eco-friendly ingredients, reducing waste, and supporting local communities. We aim to inspire and empower people to be confident, express themselves, and embrace their individuality.

HERSTORY

Koulè Cosmetics was founded in 2019 by Aleah Sandria Maisip, a now 22-year-old student at Far Eastern University of Manila under the program of Accountancy. Her passion for makeup and desire to create a positive impact on the beauty industry led her to start Koule Cosmetics, a small business based in Metro Manila, Philippines. Aleah started by creating and selling handmade eyebrow soaps to her friends and family, which eventually grew into a full-fledged cosmetic line that includes innovative products such as Powder Up Tints.

Despite her young age, Aleah's entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to her craft have led her to establish Koule Cosmetics as a sustainable and socially responsible brand that empowers people to express themselves through makeup. Her goal is to create a positive impact on the environment and support local businesses while offering high-quality and affordable cosmetic products that cater to everyone.

With the support of her team, Aleah envisions Koule Cosmetics to become a leading cosmetic brand in the Philippines that inspires and empowers people to embrace their unique beauty and express themselves through makeup. She believes that beauty should be inclusive, sustainable, and accessible to everyone, and she is committed to making this vision a reality through Koule Cosmetics.
